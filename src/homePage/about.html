Welcome to InternXP!

We are THE one-stop resource hub for CS students at CSULB, dedicated to providing students with all the resources and information they need to succeed in their academic and professional journey. From internship tips to interactive study spaces, we have got you covered! Our goal is to support you every step of the way and empower you to reach your full potential, whether you're a freshman just starting your educational journey or a senior preparing to get a job in the industry.

Meet our Developers

We are four Computer Science seniors undertaking our last semester at CSULB. At our first semester of our senior project class, when we finalized our group, we combined all our presentation ideas to create one concrete product. This is how InternXP was born. This website is a culmination of two semesters worth of planning, document creating, coding, and more; not to mention the stressful days and sleepless nights.
We hope you enjoy everything that InternXP has to offer.
